Home

Human activities are significantly altering the Earth's environment, especially freshwater systems. This includes changes in natural flow patterns and the depletion of groundwater resources. Climate change adds to these challenges. My research employs quantitative modeling to integrate climate, societal, and agricultural factors for assessing water resources and environmental impacts. Over time, my research has become more interdisciplinary, involving stakeholders, socio-economic dimensions, and data science. The focus is on addressing complex issues related to water, climate change, energy, and infrastructure resilience. This approach aims to understand these interactions better and advance water cycle studies.
